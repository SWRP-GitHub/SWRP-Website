DISCORD SERVER RULES
1: Treat everyone with respect. Keep it nice and civil for everyone
2: Try to keep things generally on-topic in the channel you're in.
3: Do not insult other members; an insult and well-intentioned ribbing aren't that hard to tell apart. Wholesome gang hours
4: Keep everything reasonably SFW. This means no NSFW.
5: No slurs or blatantly hateful language. I know it's supposed to be a joke, but if you wouldn't say it to a friend you've just made, don't say it here.
6: No political or religious debates (or b8s, m8) outside of politics. This doesn't mean what you think is political, it means anything that could be political.
7: Never ever dog your mates
8: Read.
9: No spam or self-promotion (server invites, advertisements, etc) without permission from a @Discord Moderator . This includes DMing fellow server members.
