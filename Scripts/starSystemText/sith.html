The Fragmented Sith

A product of one of the many schemes of Darth Vader, The Fragmented Sith were created by Miraxces Uduun as a secret plan to undermine the Emperor. The Acolyte of the Beyond was sent to the far reaches of Region Twelve before Imperial forces began to settle the systems; to evaluate, assess, and raise an order of Sith to be called on at a later time.

An idea that is good in theory, however the Galactic Civil War took Vader's attention far away from Region Twelve and Miraxces Uduun was left to his own devices, training the would be Sith as best as he could. Force Sensitive children were kidnapped from the various planets of Region Twelve, selected at a young age to be raised in the way of the Sith.

Those that proved merit were given Kyber crystals to bleed, harvested from the aftermath of Order 66, and instructed on how to build their own Lightsaber. Not many reached this step, most were culled from the extensive testing and training led by Uduun. By 8 ABY, the members of the Order began to test the limits of their extreme isolation, the dominos began to fall and during 9 ABY, the Order broke apart as a few believed themselves strong enough to no longer need Uduun. The Sith Temple was burned and The Fragmented Sith were born.

What are your character's goals now that they are free of Uduun's tutelage? Will they return to Acherios to salvage what they can from the ruins of the Sith Temple, will they strike out on their own in Region Twelve, or will they look for others like them to mend the shattered pieces of their fragmented Sith Order? Players looking to join the Fragmented Sith will find themselves unleashed into Region Twelve with many options before them. It is up to them to decide what they do with their learned power.
