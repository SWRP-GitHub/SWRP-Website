The New Republic Presence in Region Twelve

Presence of the burgeoning New Republic in such an isolated part of the galaxy's fringes is incredibly limited to say the least. Dealing with a plethora of issues at locations of higher importance, Region Twelve has flown under the radar of the New Republic's higher authorities. A few operatives have made their way to the distant region of space, but little has come of their efforts.

Porter Creal, one such operator, led an attempt to destabilize the region by assassinating Governor Ryehall but was caught before he was allowed to finish the mission. His actions sparked a paranoia in the Imperial leadership of Region Twelve and what few operatives remained across the various star systems in this pocket of space have gone to ground.

Before his death, Porter Creal believed his mission would be a success and a transmission was sent through backwater channels to Hosnian Prime, requesting more support. A small budget was approved for New Republic operatives to make their way to Region Twelve, but military forces cannot be supported at this time. It is up to these few operatives that are scattered around the system, or running the blockade to make their way into Region Twelve, to turn the tide of imperial influence.

How did your character get involved with the New Republic? Did they fight in the Galactic Civil War? Players looking to join the New Republic faction are fighting an uphill battle, one that will require patience, subterfuge, and the courage to fight the Empire.
