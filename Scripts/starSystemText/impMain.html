Imperial Remnant of Region Twelve

Led by the blusterous Governor Terrier Ryehall, the Imperial Remnant that hold control over Region Twelve makes its presence known throughout the systems. From various Imperial Garrisons on planets to the large Star Destroyer Decadence that sits above Marjora City, you would be hard pressed not to feel the looming shadow of Imperial authority wherever you are in Region Twelve.

The recent assassination attempt on the life of Governor Ryehall has caused the Imperial Forces to begin locking down the region, the Decadence rests above Marjora City as a show of force. Ships coming into Region Twelve submit to imperial scans or face the consequences.

The Imperial Navy is enough to maintain security and patrols and keep a lid on threats from within the Region, but they rely on their isolation from the rest of the galaxy to avoid any major conflict. The Decadence is the flagship of the Imperial Navy in Region Twelve, as it is the only Star Destroyer they have.

What happened in your character's career to get them sent to Region Twelve as a post, it's far from a prestigious station? Did they fight in the Galactic Civil War? Players looking to join the Imperial forces have a plethora of options to them, ranging from ground forces to a naval captain and anywhere in between.
