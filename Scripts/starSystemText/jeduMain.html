The Jedi Wanderers

Decades before the dark eye of the Galactic Empire ever turned to Region Twelve, there were Jedi wandering the systems that formed it. These initial guardians of peace had left the Republic alongside a group of religious devotees from the Church of the Force, hoping to guide the pilgrims safely to a new home. Inus Daxio and Malic Tedronius, Master and Apprentice, originally set their course towards a legendary mountain of kyber their passengers had seen in shared visions. As the years went by however, and the existence of such a mountain was verified, their will to stay in the sector grew along with the burgeoning Region Twelve.

For years, Jedi Master Inus Daxio trained two padawans on the slopes of Mount Matur, on the elusive seventh moon of Kiida. Malic Tedronius was the first, a pupil the High Council on Coruscant had assigned to him. The second was a student he had picked up on the nearby planet Vaedas, Aireen Sanarra, the son of the King and former heir to the throne. Eventually, his two pupils would become Jedi Knights, together protecting the region as an unstoppable force of good.

Their light however could only shine so bright while there was little darkness to challenge them. Everything changed when the Empire’s shadow was cast across the galaxy. The galactic superpower’s hatred and need to consume would eventually spread to this faraway region. First came the Inquisitors, looking for the Jedi rumored to protect the nearby worlds. Then came the Sith themselves.

Master Daxio was slaughtered by Darth Vader on the planet Vaedas, along with a great number of his allies. The silver lining for Daxio, as he lay in the snow and blood, was that by sacrificing himself he had protected the secret of Mount Matur and the existence of his students who had found refuge there.

Malic Tedronius, former protector of the region, would pass the following years in a self sentenced exile on Kiida VII. Despite his self attributed failures, he still relented to the wishes of the members of the Church of the Force when force sensitive adepts showed up seeking training. For another generation, Malic represented one of the last of the old Order as he brought up a number of pupils. His students, after leaving Kiida, would protect their new home as forces of darkness continued to spread, the light and the dark continuing their eternal battle for supremacy even in this remote region.

Eventually, Malic’s exile would end, and he’d once again leave to wander the stars, protecting the innocents still plagued by the lingering darkness. A few of his students would take up his mantle and safeguard Mount Matur, while others would begin the training of new padawans across Sector Twelve. Those called to by the force, the few who see Mount Matur in their dreams, now seek the Jedi and their allies out across the stars in hopes of getting their hands on one of the few Jedi Wayfinders.

Notable Timeline:

  • 40 BBY: The Jedi first escort the Sojourn to what would later be known as Region Twelve. >The first encounter between the Jedi and the People of Vaedas would occur, dubbed The Battle of the Vortex.


  • 37 BBY: After years of planning, Inus Daxio finally perfected the design of a Jedi Wayfinder that could lead vessels through the Daijax Nebula through to the Kiida System, and the legendary Mount Matur. He’d construct five of these Wayfinders in total. >The first Jedi Odyssey is completed when Malic Tedronius was chosen by a kyber crystal within Mount Matur.


  • 30 BBY- 13 BBY: Jedi Knights Malic Tedronius and Aireen Sanarra act as the protectors of Region Twelve, all the while continuing their tutelage under the great Inus Daxio.


  • 13 BBY: Darth Vader travels to Region Twelve, both as a personnel scouting endeavor, and as a hunt for the rumored Jedi. In a fantastical showdown on the planet Vaedas, both Daxio and the Ruler of the People of Vaedas would fall to Vader’s blade, along with their armies.


  • 13 BBY- 5 ABY: Aireen Sanarra returns to Vaedas to pick up his father’s crown, forsaking his Jedi past after his Master’s death. With him he takes a number of the Jedi Wayfinders, which he dispersed throughout the Sector for future Jedi adepts to find. >Malic Tedronius goes into exile on Kiida VII. Eventually, two Wayfinders are uncovered, and a number of apprentices start to seek the Jedi out for training. >Once their training is finished, Malic’s apprentices would wander Region Twelve, covertly defending its citizens from the Sith and from the Empire.


  • 5 ABY: Malic Tedronius ends his exile, leaving Kiida VII and disappearing into the greater galaxy. He left Mount Matur in the capable hands of some of his most qualified students.

Jedi Wanderers Today:

The students trained by Malic Tedronius still safeguard Region Twelve, creating relationships with its people by walking among them. While no official and completely organized Jedi Order has yet to be formed, the Kiida System serves as a safe haven for younger initiate's and padawan's training. Other young adepts learn from active Wanderers, studying the ways of the force as they travel across the Region. Jedi hopefuls still seek out the Jedi and their affiliates across the stars, in an attempt to find one of the elusive Wayfinders and eventually make their own way to the kyber mountain.

Notable Characters:

  • Inus Daxio- A powerful Jedi Master who was revered in the Republic for his remarkable connection to the force. Started the concept of the 'Wandering Jedi', and discovered Mount Matur -DECEASED (NPC)
  • Malic Tedronius- The former apprentice to Daxio, and father of the new generation of Wandering Jedi. The few padawans he trained now act as the primary Jedi in the region, and are responsible for training any new apprentices. -NPC
  • Aireen Sanarra- Daxio’s former second apprentice. Aireen forsake the Order after Daxio’s death, instead following in the footsteps of his father, the former King of Vaedas. -NPC
