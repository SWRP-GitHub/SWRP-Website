The star of Acherios is a weak white dwarf. The system was deemed to be of little exploitable value by the Empire during initial surveys. A large asteroid field sits in between the star and the orbit of the closes planet.
The first planet in the orbit of the star,Acherios I is an ocean world. Most of the minimal settlement that the solar system has seen can be found near the equator of the planet aboard floating towns. Fishing is the primary export of the Acherios System and its these fishing towns that provide the services. Most fishing villages have a single freighter to transport goods in and out of the town, and an empty landing pad to fit incoming vessels. The water near the poles of the planet are unstable, ranging from mountainous waves to long stretches of ice and frozen glaciers.
This arctic planet lies in a far orbit of the star. Sparse townships exist only near the few forests that have somehow survived the harsh conditions of the planet. The settlers are a hardy folk with little taste for outsiders. There is a dark secret here however, the ruins of a temple dedicated to the teaching of Sith is hidden somewhere on the planet, kept a secret by the dark acolytes that tend to the ruins. The secrets that remain here in the rubble are yet to be discovered.
Acherios is where most of the Sith that find themselves in Region Twelve will trace their origin to, and any that might look to retrace their steps to find any secrets left in the wake of the temple's burning will find themselves back here.
